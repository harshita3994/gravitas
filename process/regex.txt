regex tutorial
\d - digits
\D - anything but digits
\d{1,5} expect 1 to 5 digits
\d{5} 5 digits
\d* - digits expected
\s - Space
\S - anyting but a space
\w - character
\W - anything but a character
\w+ - w is for letters and + is for one or more
\. - To say that there is going to be a period
? - 0 or 1 repetitions
* - 0 or more repetitions
{n} - n repeats
[a-z] or [0-9] or [a-tA-f3-5] - Could include any og these characters
